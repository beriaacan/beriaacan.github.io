About Me

I am a graduate student at Istanbul Technical University, deeply passionate about high-energy particle physics and the computational tools that drive discovery. My academic journey is focused on mastering analysis workflows in ROOT/C++ and building predictive models with Python and its machine learning ecosystem. My goal is to contribute to meaningful physics analysis, particularly within the CMS experiment, by transforming complex data into clear, verifiable results.
